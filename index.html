About Me

I am Fauzi, a student studying at ITS Surabaya who has a hobby of photography. Surabaya is my birthplace. Since becoming a student in junior high school, I have enjoyed taking pictures and photographing various objects, from natural landscapes to people to objects that attract attention.
Photography for me is not just a hobby, but also a way to express myself and show the beauty of the world through the camera lens. I always try to continue to develop my photography skills and broaden the scope of knowledge about the art of photography.
